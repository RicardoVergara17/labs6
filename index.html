Our Bike Ride

After swimming in Lake Havasu on a Monday afternoon, my partner and I thought it would be a good idea to ride around the park in some bikes we rented from the city. This would not only allow us to explore the different scenery but also dry ourselves since we were still in our wet clothes. While she was having a fun time looking at the scenery, I was having fun looking at her enjoy how beautiful the scenery was. I was filled with immense gratitude and joy since I don't get to see her as often as I'd like to. The view was much better with her in it.

Our Dinner

This is the same day we finished bike riding and sun bathing at the lake.The restuarant we went to was called La Vita Dolce Italian Bistro and Lounce. She ordered the Angel Hair Pomodoro and I ordered the Filet Mignon. The night was gorgeous and so was she. The weather required no layering and the atmosphere was a chefs kiss. The only downside was exactly what we were afraid of when looking at the negative reviews was the waiter with horrible service. We had to wait a long time to be attended and served our check. Luckily we felt like nothing could ruin our night because we were just happy to be together.

Lake Havasu City

Although it is a moderately small city in Arizona, it is home of about 400 miles of shoreline. Attracting not only tourist but those who enjoy boating, fishing, and other water activites. I personally loved it here and found out it was not too far from the Grand Canyon so it is defintely on my list of places I want to visit again this year.
